##
Before you can authenticate with the TMDB API, ensure you have a TMDB account. 

# Sign Up for a TMDB Account  

- Go to [TMDB Sign Up Page](https://www.themoviedb.org/signup).
- Fill in the required details (username, email, password).
- Verify your email address by clicking on the confirmation link sent to your inbox.
- Log in to your TMDB account.

# Get Your API Key

- Go to [TMDB](https://www.themoviedb.org/) and log in to your account.
- Navigate to your **Account Settings** by clicking on your profile picture and selecting **Settings** from the dropdown menu.
